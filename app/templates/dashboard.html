Recommended daily flow

  1. Search or import prospects into the system.
  2. Open prospects and run contact discovery in bulk.
  3. Approve only real non-customers with a good fit.
  4. Preview the queue and send inside the daily limit.

What the crawler looks for

  • Visible footer or header emails
  • Contact and about pages
  • Cloudflare protected emails
  • Obfuscated public emails like `info*domain.nl`
  • Instagram, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp links
